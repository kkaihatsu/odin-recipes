French Fries Recipe

Description

A long and crispy potatoes, deep fried in high heat oil and sprinkled with salt. That's how this dish was easily prepared, everyone loves it. You can enjoy with yourself or with your family or friends.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 cup vegatable oil for frying
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water or as needed

Step by step

  1. Slice the potatoes and place them in cold water to prevent browning
  2. Sift the flour and seasonings, then gradually stir in water to make a batter
  3. Drain and dry the sliced potatoes, then dip in the batter
  4. Fry the potatoes until golden brown and crispy
