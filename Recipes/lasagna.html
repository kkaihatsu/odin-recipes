Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of the most famous dish around the world.It is originated in Naples, Italy during the Midlle Ages in the 14th century. It is a rectangular shape, with a layers of fillings that has different kind of texture and flavor. It mostly served during the holidays.

Ingredients

  • Meat(lean ground beef)
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps by step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
